How Navona Works

Navona creates personalized, AI-powered learning roadmaps to help you achieve your goals with clarity and structure.

1

Sign Up or Log In

Create your account or log in securely to access your personalized learning dashboard.

2

Enter Your Details

Provide your goal, current skill level, daily study time, and target deadline.

3

AI Generates Your Roadmap

Our AI builds a structured weekly roadmap with topics, tasks, and curated resources.

4

View & Track Progress

Follow your roadmap week by week and stay focused on what truly matters.

5

Modify Anytime

Update your preferences and regenerate your roadmap whenever your goals change.
